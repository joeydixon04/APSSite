I/DD Supports

I/DD Supports

Certified job coaching, benefits counseling, and workplace support helping individuals find and keep meaningful work.

Employment Services

  • Transportation Planning
  • Career Planning
  • Job Development
  • Supported Employment
  • Workplace Support and transition services for adults with I/DD
  • Benefits guidance on SSDI, SSI, and work incentives — so employment decisions are informed and secure

Other Supports

  • Companionship
  • Respite Care

And more — Connect with AnchorPoint Supports to discuss your needs.

Non-Medical Supports for Seniors

Compassionate, attentive support helping older adults stay comfortable, connected, and confident.

Companionship

Meaningful social connection and presence — helping seniors stay engaged and supported in daily life.

Technology Assistance

Help with phones, tablets, video calls, and everyday technology so seniors can stay connected with family and services.

Transportation

Reliable rides to appointments, errands, and community activities.

Respite Care

Temporary relief for family caregivers — giving you time to recharge while your loved one is in good hands.
