Trusted Relationship-Driven I/DD Supports and Non-Medical Supports for Seniors

AnchorPoint Supports provides credentialed Employment Services, credentialed Work Incentive Benefits Planning services, and other Individualized Services for Individuals with I/DD, along with compassionate Non-Medical Supports for Seniors across Johnson County, Kansas — helping individuals and families move forward with confidence.

I/DD Supports: Employment Services, Benefits Planning, Transportation Planning, Respite Care, and Transition Support.

Non-Medical Supports for Seniors: Companionship, Transportation, Respite Care, Technology Assistance, and more.

Our relationship-driven small-provider model

We believe in deep relationships and personalized support. Here's how we work with you.

Founder & Director

Scott Dixon

"My mission is simple: to provide reliable, compassionate support for individuals and families navigating everyday life and important transitions."

As a father of two sons with developmental disabilities, I have personally navigated many of the same systems, challenges, and decisions that families face every day. I also spent three years as a full-time caregiver for a loved one with cancer.

That lived experience, combined with my professional background in Employment Services and Benefits Planning, inspired me to found AnchorPoint Supports.

I understand both the structure of the system and the real-world needs of the people who rely on it.

"We intentionally keep our client base small, because every individual and family we serve deserves meaningful attention — not just a spot on a schedule."

— AnchorPoint Supports, LLC

Credentials

Scott holds nationally recognized credentials that help ensure every client receives knowledgeable, professional, and person-centered guidance.

CESP™
Credentialed Employment Support Professional™
Nationally recognized credential for specialists supporting individuals with disabilities in finding and keeping meaningful employment.
WIP-C™
Work Incentive Practitioner – Credentialed
Cornell University’s Yang-Tan Institute on Employment and Disability. Credentialed in SSDI/SSI benefits counseling and work incentive planning.
Coming Soon — July 2026
Youth-C™
Youth – Credentialed
Guidance with Social Security disability benefits and work incentives as they apply to youth — with a focus on education, careers, and benefits for young adults with disabilities.

AnchorPoint Supports is an independent business and is not affiliated with or endorsed by APSE or Cornell University.
